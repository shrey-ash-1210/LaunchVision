PSLV Launch Vehicle

The Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) is one of the most successful launch vehicles developed by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). It is known for its versatility and reliability in launching satellites into various orbits.

Height: 44 meters

Mass: 320 tons (Lift-off)

Stages: Three stages

Payload Capacity:

  • GTO (Geosynchronous Transfer Orbit): Up to 1425 kg
  • LEO (Low Earth Orbit): Up to 3800 kg
