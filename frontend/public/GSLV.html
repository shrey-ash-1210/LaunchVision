GSLV Launch Vehicle

The GSLV is a launch vehicle developed by ISRO to deploy satellites into geosynchronous transfer orbits.Known for its ability to carry heavier payloads and has been a crucial part of India's space missions since its inception.

Height: 49 meters

Mass: 414.75 tons (Lift-off)

Stages: Three stages

Payload Capacity:

  • GTO (Geosynchronous Transfer Orbit): Up to 2500 kg
  • LEO (Low Earth Orbit): Up to 5000 kg
